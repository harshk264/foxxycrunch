Our story

From Lotus Ponds to Your Snack Bowl

Foxxy was born out of one simple idea — snacking shouldn't be a trade-off between tasty and healthy. So we went to the source: the lotus ponds of Mithila, where the world's best makhana grows.

Every Foxxy pack is popped fresh, slow-roasted in small batches and tossed in spice blends made just for us. The fox on the pack? That's you — the smart one who figured out a snack can crunch like junk and care like a superfood.
