The brand

From Mithila's Lotus Ponds to the Modern Shelf

Foxxy was built on one idea — snacking shouldn't be a trade-off between tasty and healthy. We source makhana from the lotus ponds of Mithila, slow-roast it (never fry it) and season it in bold desi flavours that drive repeat purchase.

For trade partners that means a brand with a clear consumer story, distinctive packaging and a product that moves — backed by a team focused on consistent quality and dependable fulfilment.

  • ✓ Makhana sourced from growers in Bihar's Mithila belt
  • ✓ Slow-roasted, not fried — clean-label, no palm oil, no maida
  • ✓ Distinctive, retail-ready brand identity & packaging
  • ✓ Operated by a registered LLP, focused on trade partnerships
